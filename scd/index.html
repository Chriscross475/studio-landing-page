About the game

Super Castle Defenders is a bright, toon-styled castle-defense roguelite. Build and upgrade your towers, command your hero, and hold the line as wave after wave crashes against your walls. Every level-up offers a choice of rewards — Arcane Beam, Gold Rush, Gold Fury and more — so no two runs play the same.
